Nuxbe - Your Mobile ERP System

Nuxbe brings the power of your ERP system to your smartphone. Manage your business from anywhere - in the office, on the road, or on-site with customers.

KEY FEATURES:

- Order Management
Create and edit quotes, orders, and invoices directly on your mobile device. Keep track of all orders in real-time.

- Customer Management
Access your customer data anytime. Contact information, order history, and communication in one place.

- Project Management
Track project progress, record work hours, and document project details directly on-site.

- Inventory Management
Check stock levels, record stock movements, and keep track of your items.

- Time Tracking
Record work hours on the go and assign them directly to projects or customers.

- Document Management
Access important documents and files directly from your mobile device.

BENEFITS:

- Mobile access to your complete ERP system
- Intuitive operation, optimized for smartphones
- Real-time data synchronization
- Secure and GDPR compliant
- Perfect for field service and mobile teams

Nuxbe is a comprehensive ERP system specifically developed for modern business processes. The mobile app provides seamless access to your business data on the go. By Team Nifty GmbH - your partner for professional business software.
